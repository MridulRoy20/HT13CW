Places to visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO world heritage sites.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often reffered to as the Pink city known for its historic sites, including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City palace

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its serene backwaters and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cusine

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a creamy and flavourfu indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a frangnent and aromatic rice dish with various regional variations. Notable ingrediants include:

  1. Basmati Rice
  2. Chicken or lamb(or vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices(such as cumin, coriander and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flavour and color.

Dosa

Dosa is a South Indian crispy pancake with fermented rice and lentil batter.
